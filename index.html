About Us

Philosophy

Intelligence is not about the information collected, but rather the positive impact it has on targeted business processes and outcomes.

Motivation

To assist our clients in establishing and enhancing their intelligence methodologies, organizations, and tools to ensure they have the greatest likelihood of success, what ever their business endeavours.

Services

We offer our clients support from understanding and implementing the basics of intelligence through to the complex integration of intelligence support and targeted business operations. Our products and services include:

Intelligence

Advice on new and effective intelligence planning, analysis, and dissemination capabilities and processes, and the efficient and effective integration of intelligence collection capabilities and methods to maximize the probability of successful intelligence operations.

Training

Training support for both intelligence and targeting, including education and exercises.

Doctrine

Capturing business principles, tenets, and processes in a clear and articulate format.

Targeting

Assistance and advice on the full range of targeting issues from, selection of desired outcomes, operational guidance, target selection and development, planning and review processes.
